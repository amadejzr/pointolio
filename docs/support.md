# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 2026_

Pointolio respects your privacy. The app is designed to work fully offline and does not collect, store, or transmit personal data.

## Data Collection

Pointolio does **not** collect any personal information.

- No accounts
- No analytics
- No tracking
- No ads
- No third-party services

All game data (players, scores, games) is stored **locally on your device only**.

## Internet Usage

Pointolio does not require an internet connection to function.  
The app does not communicate with any servers.

## Sharing Content

When you choose to share a scorecard, Pointolio generates an image **locally on your device** and presents it using the system share sheet.

- The image is created on-device
- The image is shared only when you explicitly choose to share
- Pointolio does not store, upload, or receive shared content

Any sharing destination (messages, social apps, etc.) is handled entirely by the operating system and the selected app.

## Permissions

Pointolio does not request access to:
- Contacts
- Location
- Camera
- Microphone
- Photos (unless required by the system for sharing)

## Children’s Privacy

Pointolio does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If the app’s behavior changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated accordingly.
