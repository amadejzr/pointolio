# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 2026

Pointolio respects your privacy. This app is designed to work fully offline and does **not collect, store, or share any personal data**.

---

## Data Collection

Pointolio does **not** collect any personal information.

- No accounts
- No sign-in
- No analytics
- No ads
- No tracking
- No network requests

All data stays on your device.

---

## Local Storage

Pointolio stores game data **locally on your device only** using an internal database (Drift).

This includes:
- Game names
- Player names
- Scores
- Game history
- App settings

This data is never transmitted outside your device.

---

## Sharing Content

When you choose to share a game result, Pointolio generates an **image** (scorecard) on your device.

- The image is created locally
- You decide where and how to share it
- Pointolio does not access or track shared content

---

## Third-Party Services

Pointolio does **not** use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

---

## Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally:
- Deleting the app removes all stored data
- No data remains on any server
